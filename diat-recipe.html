Dosa

Dosa

Preparation & Cooking: 15-20 mins
Serves persons:5-6

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cup rice
  2. 3/4 cup urad dal
  3. 1 tbsp maida
  4. salt

Potato filling:

  1. 2 large boiled potato
  2. 1 medium onions sliced
  3. 2 green chillies
  4. 1/4 tsp mustard seed
  5. 1/2 tsp urad dal
  6. 1/2 tsp ginger grated
  7. 2-3 curry leaves
  8. 3 tbsp finely chopped coriander
  9. 1/2 tsp turmeric
  10. salt to taste
  11. 1/2 tsp sugar(optional)
  12. oil for frying

Coconut chutney:

  1. 1 cup grated coconut
  2. 2-3 green chilli
  3. 1 small piece ginger
  4. 3 tsp roasted split chickpeas
  5. 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  6. salt to taste
  7. sugar to taste
  8. 3 tbsp coriander chopped

Method:

  1. Soak Rice and Urad dal for 4 to 5 hours.
  2. Grind it to paste adding water as required. Then add maida and salt grind again.
  3. Cover and keep aside in a warm place for overnight.
  4. Heat pan with little oil and pour a ladle of batter, spread in circular motion.
  5. Let it cook for 3 to 4mins.
  6. Put some ghee/butter on it and Serve it with coconut chutney and Alu sabji.

Potato Filling:

  1. Cut the potatoes in small pieces.
  2. Heat oil in an pan and add urad dal when dal become golden brown then add mustard.
  3. Then add curry leaves, green chillies, onions and ginger and fry for 2 minute.
  4. Add salt, turmeric and finally add potato.
  5. Mix it well and garnish with coriander leaves.

Coconut chutney:

  1. Grind all the ingredient in coconut chutney with little water.
  2. Give tadka heat pan, put 1tsp oil, mustard and 2-3 curry leaves.
  3. When mustard start crackling, add that tadka to chutney.
  4. Our coconut chutney is ready to serve with dosa.

Oats Upma

Oats Upma

Preparation & Cooking: 35-40 mins
Serves 4-5 persons

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup old fashioned oats
  2. 1 small onion finely chopped
  3. 4-5 Beans thinly sliced
  4. 1/2 Carrot thinly sliced
  5. 1/2 cup Cabbage thinly sliced
  6. 2-3 green chilli sliced
  7. 2 tsp green peas
  8. 1 tsp garlic paste
  9. 3-4 curry leaves
  10. 2 tbsp coriander leaves
  11. 1/4 mustard seeds
  12. 1/2 inch cinnamon
  13. 2 cloves
  14. 1 cardamom
  15. salt to taste
  16. 3tbsp oil

Method:

  1. Dry roast the old fashioned oats till get aroma from oats.
  2. Grind the cinnamon, cloves and cardamom keep aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and then add mustard, cumin, urad dal and fry till dals turns golden brown.
  4. Now add curry leaves, green chilli, onion, beans, carrot and peas and saute till veggies become soft.
  5. Then add cabbage, salt, sugar and pinch of grind masala powder, mix it well.Then add water to boil.
  6. Now add carefully oats to boiling water and keep stirring for 2 minutes.
  7. Close the lid and reduce the heat. Let it cook for 4-5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves.

Diat Sandwich

Diat Sandwich

Preparation & Cooking: 5-10 mins
Serves persons: 2-3

Ingredients:

  1. 6 slices whole-wheat bread
  2. 1 extra ripe avocado
  3. 8 leaves iceburg lettuce
  4. 1 small onion sliced
  5. 1 tomato sliced
  6. 1 cucumber sliced
  7. 1 capsicum sliced
  8. 2 tbsp lemon juice
  9. 1/4 tsp pepper powder
  10. salt to taste

Method:

  1. Combine avocado, lemon juice, salt, pepper powder and mash until smooth spread, Keep aside.
  2. Toast bread and then spread an equal amount of avacado spread on each slice.
  3. Now on 3 bread slices put lettuce, onion, tomato, cucumber.
  4. Cover each with another slice of toast. Cut sandwiches in half and serve immediately.
  5. This raita goes well with all type of sabji and rice.
