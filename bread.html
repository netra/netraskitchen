Spinach Paratha

Preparation & Cooking: 15-20 mins
Serves 4-5 persons

Ingredients:

  1. 1 bunch palak finely chopped
  2. 2 cup whole wheat flour
  3. 2 tsp red chili powder
  4. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  5. 1 tsp cumin powder
  6. 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  7. 1 tsp cumin
  8. 1 tsp oregano(ajwan)
  9. 1 tsp garlic paste
  10. salt to taste
  11. oil for frying
  12. 1 cup water

Method:

  1. Heat the oil in pan and splutter jeera in it. Add finely cut palak saute for 2 minutes.
  2. Mix the palak in flour along with other ingredients except oil.
  3. First mix the flour well with spinach and then add water to make just like chapathi dough
  4. Make small balls out of dough and Pat it down with flour. Roll out circle shape little thicker parathas.
  5. Place on preheated pan, add some oil all the side.Cook the parathas both sides until golden spots appears.
  6. Serve hot with pickle or raita.

Aloo Paratha

Aloo Paratha

Preparation & Cooking: 15-20 mins
Serves 4-5 persons

Ingredients:

  1. 2-3 medium boiled potato
  2. 2 cup whole wheat flour
  3. 2 tsp red chili powder
  4. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  5. 1 tsp cumin powder
  6. 1/2 coriander powder
  7. 1/2 amchoor powder
  8. 1 tsp cumin
  9. 1 tsp oregano(ajwan)
  10. 1 tsp garlic/ginger paste
  11. salt to taste
  12. oil for frying
  13. 1 cup water

Method:

  1. Add water in flour and make soft dough. Set aside, make the filling.
  2. Mix all ingredients except oil and mash it well. Make small ball out of the mix.
  3. Make small balls out of dough and Pat it down with flour.
  4. Now roll out half way and keep the stuffing ball inside, Seal the ends towards centre.
  5. Dust this in flour and roll out circle shape little thicker parathas.
  6. Place on preheated pan, add some oil all the side.Cook the parathas both sides until golden spots appears.
  7. Serve hot with pickle or raita.

Mooli Paratha

Mooli Paratha

Preparation & Cooking: 20-25 mins
Serves 4-5 persons

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup Radish grated
  2. 2 cup whole wheat flour
  3. 1 finely chopped green chili
  4. 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  5. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  6. 1 tsp cumin
  7. 1 tsp oregano(ajwan)
  8. 1/2 tsp garlic paste
  9. 3 tbsp finely chopped coriander leaves
  10. salt to taste
  11. oil for frying

Method:

  1. Add water in flour and make soft dough. Set aside, make the stuffiing.
  2. Add little salt to grated mooli, keep that aside, later squeeze the water from it.
  3. Heat the oil in pan and splutter jeera, add finely chopped chilly, garlic saute for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the squeezed radish and other ingredientes, mix and fry well till radish becomes dry.
  5. When its cool, make small balls out of mix.
  6. Make same size balls out of dough and Pat it down with flour.
  7. Now roll out half way and keep the stuffing ball inside, Seal the ends towards centre.
  8. Dust this in flour and roll out circle shape little thicker parathas.
  9. Place on preheated pan, add some oil all the side.Cook the parathas both sides until golden spots appears.
  10. Serve hot with pickle or raita.

Bhatura

Bhatura

Preparation & Cooking: 20-25 mins
Serves persons:3-4

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cup whole wheat flour
  2. 1 cup maida
  3. 2 tbsp yoghurt
  4. 2 tsp sugar
  5. 2 tsp salt
  6. 1/2 baking soda
  7. 1 tbsp oil or butter
  8. 1/2 cup warm water

Method:

  1. Its better to start bhatura dough the night before.
  2. In wide large bowl add maida, sugar, baking soda and yoghurt. Mix it well and cover with a cloth for overnight fermentation.
  3. Next day, in other bowl add wheat flour,salt and fermented dough.Mix it well by warm water.
  4. Knead the dought by appling ghee or oil for 5 to 10 minutes.Keep covered for 3 hours.
  5. Make 10-12 balls and roll them into 5 inch discs.
  6. Deep fry in really hot oil.
  7. Serve hot with chole.
