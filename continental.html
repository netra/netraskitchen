Diat Sandwich

Preparation & Cooking: 5-10 mins
Serves persons: 2-3

Ingredients:

  1. 6 slices whole-wheat bread
  2. 1 extra ripe avocado
  3. 8 leaves iceburg lettuce
  4. 1 small onion sliced
  5. 1 tomato sliced
  6. 1 cucumber sliced
  7. 1 capsicum sliced
  8. 2 tbsp lemon juice
  9. 1/4 tsp pepper powder
  10. salt to taste

Method:

  1. Combine avocado, lemon juice, salt, pepper powder and mash until smooth spread, Keep aside.
  2. Toast bread and then spread an equal amount of avacado spread on each slice.
  3. Now on 3 bread slices put lettuce, onion, tomato, cucumber.
  4. Cover each with another slice of toast. Cut sandwiches in half and serve immediately.
  5. This raita goes well with all type of sabji and rice.
