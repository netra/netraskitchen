Dosa

Preparation & Cooking: 15-20 mins
Serves persons:5-6

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cup rice
  2. 3/4 cup urad dal
  3. 1 tbsp maida
  4. salt

Potato filling:

  1. 2 large boiled potato
  2. 1 medium onions sliced
  3. 2 green chillies
  4. 1/4 tsp mustard seed
  5. 1/2 tsp urad dal
  6. 1/2 tsp ginger grated
  7. 2-3 curry leaves
  8. 3 tbsp finely chopped coriander
  9. 1/2 tsp turmeric
  10. salt to taste
  11. 1/2 tsp sugar(optional)
  12. oil for frying

Coconut chutney:

  1. 1 cup grated coconut
  2. 2-3 green chilli
  3. 1 small piece ginger
  4. 3 tsp roasted split chickpeas
  5. 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  6. salt to taste
  7. sugar to taste
  8. 3 tbsp coriander chopped

Method:

  1. Soak Rice and Urad dal for 4 to 5 hours.
  2. Grind it to paste adding water as required. Then add maida and salt grind again.
  3. Cover and keep aside in a warm place for overnight.
  4. Heat pan with little oil and pour a ladle of batter, spread in circular motion.
  5. Let it cook for 3 to 4mins.
  6. Put some ghee/butter on it and Serve it with coconut chutney and Alu sabji.

Potato Filling:

  1. Cut the potatoes in small pieces.
  2. Heat oil in an pan and add urad dal when dal become golden brown then add mustard.
  3. Then add curry leaves, green chillies, onions and ginger and fry for 2 minute.
  4. Add salt, turmeric and finally add potato.
  5. Mix it well and garnish with coriander leaves.

Coconut chutney:

  1. Grind all the ingredient in coconut chutney with little water.
  2. Give tadka heat pan, put 1tsp oil, mustard and 2-3 curry leaves.
  3. When mustard start crackling, add that tadka to chutney.
  4. Our coconut chutney is ready to serve with dosa.

Rava Dosa

Rava Dosa

Preparation & Cooking: 15-20 mins
Servne persons: 3-4

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup fine rava
  2. 1/2 cup rice flour
  3. 1/4 cup maida
  4. 2 small onions finely chopped
  5. 3 tbsp finely chopped coriander
  6. 1 tsp green chilli paste or finely chopped green chillies
  7. 1 tsp cumin seeds
  8. 1/2 tsp turmeric
  9. salt to taste
  10. 1/2 tsp sugar(optional)
  11. oil for frying
  12. 2 cups water

Method:

  1. Mix all the ingredients except oil to make batter. The batter should have a thin consistency and keep this aside for 5-10 mins.
  2. Heat pan with little oil. Check the consistency of batter as the rava absorbs water, add more water such that it is of pouring consistency (like thick buttermilk)
  3. Pour a ladle of batter, spread in circular motion. Let it cook for 2 mins and flip it. Cook it 2mins or until crisp.
  4. Serve it with Mint chutney and Alu sabji.

Utappa

Utappam

Preparation & Cooking: 15-20 mins
Serves persons: 4-5

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cup of leftover idli/dosa batter
  2. 1/2 cup wheat flour
  3. 2 tbsp rice flour
  4. 1 small onion finely chopped
  5. 1 small tomato finely chopped
  6. 2-3 green chillies thinly sliced
  7. 2 tbsp coriander finely chopped
  8. 1/2 cumin seeds
  9. 2 tsp sugar
  10. Salt to taste
  11. 2 tbsp oil

Method:

  1. Heat oil in pan and add cumin, onion, chillies and fry of 2 minutes.
  2. Then add tomato,salt and sugar saute for minute, keep aside and allow to cool.
  3. Take leftover batter and add wheat flour,rice flour and little water mix well.
  4. Then Add masala tadka to batter and mix well.
  5. Heat pan with little oil and pour a ladle of batter, spread in circular motion.
  6. Let it cook both side for 3 to 4mins.
  7. Put some ghee/butter on it and Serve it with coconut chutney and Alu sabji.

Idli

Idli

Preparation & Cooking: 15-20 mins
Serves persons:5-6

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cup Idli Rava
  2. 1 cup Urad Daal
  3. 1/2 cup cooked rice
  4. Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Soak idli rava and urad dal separately for 4-5 hours.
  2. Grind urad dal to paste adding water as required. Then add cooked rice and salt grind again.
  3. Takeout water from rava and mix with grind paste. Beat batter for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Cover and keep aside in a warm place for overnight.
  5. Grease the idli plate and add spoonful of the batter to each of the idli plates.
  6. Put idli stand in idli cooker and cook for 10-15 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with sambar and coconut chutney.

Rava Idli

Rava Idli

Preparation & Cooking: 15-20 mins
Serves persons:3-4

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup Rava
  2. 2 cup yogurt
  3. 2 tbsp coriander finely chopped
  4. 1/4 tsp baking soda
  5. Salt to taste

For Tadka

  1. 1 tsp Urad dal
  2. 1/2 tsp chana dal
  3. 1 tsp Mustard seeds
  4. 1/2 tsp ginger grated
  5. 8-10 Cashew pieces
  6. 1-2 Green chillies finely chopped
  7. 2-3 Curry leaves
  8. Asafetida a pinch
  9. 1 tbsp Oil

Method:

  1. Heat oil in pan and add Urad dal, chana dal, mustard seeds to splutter.
  2. Then add curry leaves, asafetida, green chillies, garlic, cashew pieces and mix well.
  3. Now add rava and fry until it turns light brown. keep aside and allow to cool.
  4. Then add yogurt,salt to rava. Mix it well.
  5. Now add baking soda coriander leaves to this and mix well. Keep aside for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Afer the 20 minutes if batter is thick then add little water to thin it out.
  7. Grease the idli plate and add spoonful of the batter to each of the idli plates.
  8. Put idli stand in idli cooker for 10-15 minutes.
  9. Serve hot with ghee and potato curry and coconut chutney.

Masala Upma

Masala Upma

Preparation & Cooking: 35-40 mins
Serves persons: 4-5

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup Rava
  2. 1 small onion finely chopped
  3. 4-5 Beans thinly sliced
  4. 1/2 Carrot thinly sliced
  5. 1/2 cup Cabbage thinly sliced
  6. 2-3 green chilli sliced
  7. 2 tsp green peas
  8. 1 tsp garlic paste
  9. 3-4 curry leaves
  10. 2 tbsp coriander leaves
  11. 1/4 mustard seeds
  12. 1/2 inch cinnamon
  13. 2 cloves
  14. 1 cardamom
  15. salt to taste
  16. 3 tbsp oil

Method:

  1. Heat gee/oil in pan and then add rava, roast the rave until it gives nice aroma.
  2. Grind the cinnamon, cloves and cardamom keep aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and then add mustard, cumin, urad dal and fry till dals turns golden brown.
  4. Now add curry leaves, green chilli, onion, beans, carrot and peas and saute till veggies become soft.
  5. Then add cabbage, salt, sugar and pinch of grind masala powder, mix it well.Then add water to boil.
  6. Now add carefully rava to boiling water and keep stirring for 2 minutes.
  7. Close the lid and reduce the heat. Let it cook for 4-5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves.

Diet Sandwich

Diet Sandwich

Preparation & Cooking: 5-10 mins
Serves persons: 2-3

Ingredients:

  2. 1 extra ripe avocado
  3. 8 leaves iceburg lettuce
  4. 1 small onion sliced
  5. 1 tomato sliced
  6. 1 cucumber sliced

Pav Bhaji


Pav Bhaji

Preparation & Cooking: 35-40 mins
Serves 3-4 persons

Ingredients:

  1. 2 big potato boiled
  2. 2 medium onion
  3. 2 small tomato
  4. 1 capsicum
  5. 10-15 beans
  6. 2 tsp ginger and garlic paste
  7. 1/2 cup peas
  8. 1/2 turmeric powder
  9. 1tsp chilli powder
  10. 1tbsp pav bhaji masala
  11. 2tsp chopped coriander
  12. salt to taste
  13. 3 tbsp oil

Method:

  1. Heat oil in pan, add finely chopped onion, beans, capsicum fry for 8-10 minutes.
  2. Then add garlic, ginger paste saute till row smell disappear.
  3. Add the finely chopped tomato and fry till the oil separates.
  4. Add turmeric powder, chilli powder, salt and pav bhaji masala and mix well.
  5. Add boiled and mashed carrot and potato, mix well.
  6. Now add 1 cup water, boil for 5-10 minutes.
  7. Finely garnish with chopped coriander.
  8. slice the pav and put little butter on both side
  9. Heat tava and cook pav on both sides.

Hakka Noodle

Hakka Noodle

Preparation & Cooking: 15-20 mins
Serves persons: 4-5

Ingredients:

  1. 1 pack hakka noodles
  2. 1 bunch spring onion chopped
  3. 2 small carrot thinly sliced
  4. 1 cup capsicum thinly sliced
  5. 1 cup cabbage thinly sliced
  6. 5-6 baby corns
  7. 2 tsp garlic fine chopped
  8. 2 tsp tomato ketchup
  9. 1 tsp red chilli sauce
  10. 1 tsp Pepper powder
  11. 1 tsp soya sauce
  12. 1/2 tsp vinegar
  13. pinch of ajjinomotto(optional)
  14. Salt to taste
  15. 3 tbsp Oil for fry

Method:

  1. Cook noodles as per the instruction given in the pack and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add spring onion white part, ginger and garlic, stir fry till it starts browning.
  3. Now add carrot, capsicum and cabbage, baby corn in order one by one, and frying each for minute.
  4. Then add soya sauce, vinegar, pepper powder, red chilli sauce, tomato ketchup and salt, mix well.
  5. Add the cooked noodles and mix well.Then add spring onion green part, stir it in high flame.
  6. Serve hot.

Vada Pav

Vada Pav

Preparation & Cooking: 35-40 mins
Serves 4-5 persons

ingredients for vada:

  1. 6-7 pav
  2. 4 large Potato, boiled and mashed
  3. 1 tsp green chilli paste
  4. 1 tsp garlic paste
  5. 1/2 garlic paste
  6. 1 tsp finely chopped coriander
  7. salt to taste
  8. 1/4 Asafoetida
  9. 1 tbsp oil
  10. 1 tsp turmeric powder
  11. 1/2 cup Besan(chickpea flour)
  12. 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  13. 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  14. Oil for frying

For dry coconut Chutney:

  1. 1/2 cup Grated Dried Coconut
  2. 2-3 Garlic cloves peeled
  3. 1/2 cumin seeds
  4. 2 tbsp Red chilli powder
  5. 2 curry leaves
  6. salt
  7. 1/4 sugar(optional)

For Green Chutney

  1. 2 cup chopped coriander
  2. 1/2 cup chopped mint
  3. 1/2 cumin seeds
  4. 1 tsp peanut, roasted
  5. 1-2 green chilli
  6. Pinch of salt
  7. 2 tbsp lemon juice
  8. 2 tbsp water

Method:

  1. To make coconut chutney grind all ingredients, make fine powder.
  2. To make green chutney grind all ingredients to a fine paste.
  3. Slice pav and sprinkle 1tsp coconut chutney on bottom half of bun.
  4. Spread green chutney onto top half of bun, keep it aside.
  5. Heat oil in pan, add asafoetida,green chilli paste, ginger and garlic paste saute for a minute.
  6. Add salt, turmeric powder and then add mashed potato, chopped coriander and mix well
  7. Make small balls out of potato mix.keep aside.
  8. In a bowl, combine chickpea, 1/4tsp turmeric powder, chilli powder, pinch of salt, baking soda and 1 cup water. Mix well.
  9. Make a little thin batter, and dip potato balls in batter to coat well.
  10. Deep fry in hot oil till golden brown.
  11. Place one vada in pav and serve hot.

Spring Roll

Spring Roll

Preparation & Cooking: 15-20 mins
Serves persons

Ingredients:

  1. 10-12 Spring roll wrappers
  2. 2-3 spring onion
  3. 4-5 Beans thin sliced
  4. 1 small carrot thin sliced
  5. 1/2 capsicum thin sliced
  6. 1/2 cup cabbage shredded
  7. 1 tsp Pepper powder
  8. 3-4 tsp soya sauce
  9. 2-3 tsp vinegar
  10. Salt to taste
  11. 3 tbsp Oil for fry

Method:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add spring onion white part, ginger and garlic, stir fry till it starts browning.
  2. Now add carrot, beans, capsicum and cabbage in order one by one, and frying each for minute.
  3. Then add soya sauce, vinegar, pepper powder and salt, mix well.
  4. Add spring onion green part, stir it in high flame.
  5. Spring roll stuffing is ready.
  6. Defrost spring roll sheet to room temperature and then separate the sheet carefully.
  7. Take one sheet and keep 2tbsp of stuffing a little above one corner.
  8. Fold in the end of the corner and then both sides roll in such a way to cover the stuffing until half way.
  9. Make a paste of maida with little water and use that to line the corners of the sheets.
  10. Again roll tightly to make it completely sealed.
  11. Repeat the process to finish the stuffing.
  12. Now deep fry the spring roll.

Masala Vada

Masala Vada

Preparation & Cooking: 15-20 mins
Serves persons: 4-5

Ingredients:

  1. 1/2 cup channa dal
  2. 1/2 cup moong dal
  3. 1 tbsp rice flour/rava
  4. 2 garlic clove
  5. 1/2 inch ginger
  6. 2 green chili
  7. 1 tsp cumin seeds
  8. 2 tbsp mint chopped
  9. 2 tsp chopped coriander
  10. Salt to taste
  11. Oil for frying

Method:

  1. Soak channa dal and moong dal for 2 or 3 hrs.Then drain the water completely and keep aside
  2. Grind garlic, ginger, cumin seeds, green chili, mint, corinder and salt to paste.
  3. Now grind chana dal and moong dal to coarsely(not too smooth not very coarse too)
  4. Tranfer this mixture to a bowl and add chopped onion, sooji,grind masala paste and mix well.
  5. Then wet both hands and make equal sized ball out of the mixture, and pat them on your pam to make a circle to medim thickness.
  6. Deep fry in hot oil until golden in color, flipping once in between. Always cook in medium flame and drain in paper towel

Bhel Puri

Bhel Puri

Preparation & Cooking: 15-20 mins
Serves persons

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup puffed rice
  2. 1 small Potato, boiled and mashed
  3. 1 small onion finely chopped
  4. 1 small tomato finely chopped
  5. 2 green chilies chopped
  6. 1 tbsp roasted peanuts
  7. 1/2 cup sev
  8. 1/2 cup nimkis
  9. 1/2 cup gol gappas
  10. 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  11. 5 tbsp mint and corinder chutney
  12. 2 tbsp tamarind chutney
  13. 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  14. 1/2 tsp chopped coriander

Method:

  1. Mix the puffed rice, onion, chili, tomato, potato and mix it well.
  2. Lightly crush the nimkis and golgappas. Then mix all other ingredients.
  3. Finally Garnish with coriander and serve immediately.
