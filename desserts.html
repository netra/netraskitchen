Kalakand

Preparation & Cooking: 15-20 mins
Serves persons: 4-5

Ingredients:

  1. 15oz whole milk ricotta cheese
  2. 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  3. 6-8 pistachios sliced
  4. 3-4 almonds

Method:

  1. Take a microwave bowl,grease withe little ghee.
  2. Now add the ricotta cheese and condensed milk, mix it continuously till cheese get mixed well.
  3. When its done, its looks as creamy paste.
  4. Then put this in microwave,and keep the microwave in high temperature for 5 minutes.
  5. Keep an eye on the mixture so it does not boil over or it may double their volume.
  6. Take out bowl, stir the mixer very well and again put the bowl in microwave for 2 minutes.
  7. But you need to keep on peering into your microwave and at any sign of the mixture spilling over, stop -stir and start again.
  8. Keep on repeat it till mixture consistency should be thick, grainy and little light brown in color.(It normally take 15-20 minutes)
  9. Now transfer to a plat and sprinkle sliced pistachios and almonds on the top.
  10. Keep aside and once they are cooled, cut into square shape.

Banana Bread

Preparation & Cooking: 75-80 mins
Serves persons: 8-10

Ingredients:

  1. 3 cup of all-purpose flour or maida
  2. 3 egg
  3. 2 cup sugar
  4. 2 ripe banana
  5. 1 1/2 cup oil
  6. 1 tsp baking soda
  7. 1 tsp salt
  8. 1/2 cup sliced walnut
  9. 1 tsp venilla essence
  10. 1 tsp cinamon (optinal)

Method:

  1. Pre heat the oven to 350°F(175°C).
  2. Mash the bananas in large mixing bowl.
  3. Then add sugar, egg, oil, salt, baking powder, sliced walnuts, venilla essence in order one by one and Mixing each for a minute with fork.
  4. Then add all purpose flour, mix it well.
  5. Finely pour this mixture into a grease 4x8 inch loaf pan.
  6. Bake it in pre-heated oven for 1 hour.
  7. Remove from pan and slice it to serve.

Mango Mousse

Preparation & Cooking: 15-20 mins
Serves persons: 6-8

Ingredients:

  1. 1 can (750ml) mango pulp
  2. 500ml whipping cream
  3. 2 packages (15ml) mango flavoured gelatin
  4. 1 cup water
  5. 1 cup chopped strawberry

Method:

  1. Put the mango pulp into bowl and heat it low temp.
  2. Now put water and gelatin and stir it well.
  3. When this mixture become little hot, keep this aside for cooling.
  4. Whisk the whipping cream with an electric whisk until they form soft peaks.
  5. Put this whipping cream to mango mixture and slowly mix it.
  6. Put into a large serving bowl or individual bowls and leave to set in refrigerator for at least 3 hours.
