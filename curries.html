Kadai Paneer

Kadai Paneer

Preparation & Cooking: 35-40 mins
Serves persons: 4-5

Ingredients:

  1. 250 gms paneer
  2. 2 green capsicum (square cut)
  3. 1 onion (square cut)
  4. 2tsp ginger and garlic paste
  5. 3-4 tomatoes chopped
  6. 3-4 green chilies.
  7. 1tsp coriander seeds roasted and crushed
  8. 4-5 red chilies crushed
  9. 1/2 cumin powder
  10. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  11. 2tsp kasuri menthi
  12. 1/2 cup chopped coriander
  13. salt to taste
  14. 3tbsp oil

Method:

  1. Heat 2 tsp oil in pan and shallow fry paneer and keep aside.
  2. In same pan fry capsicum and onion separately keep aside.
  3. In other pan heat oil, add garlic, ginger paste saute till the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add chopped tomato, fry still they become paste.
  5. Now add salt, coriander powder,cumin powder,crushed chilly powder,turmeric powder and green chilly.
  6. Mix them well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Add capsicum, onion and paneer, mix it well.
  8. Finally add the kasuri methi and coriander, stir it and cook 2 more min's.
  9. Kadai paneer is ready to served with roti,pulka and naan

Malai Kofta

Malai Kofta

Preparation & Cooking: 35-45 mins
Serves persons: 4-5

Ingredients:

  1. 1 onion
  2. 4 medium tomatoes
  3. 1 tsp ginger paste
  4. 1 tsp garlic paste
  5. 2 green chilies
  6. 1 tsp cumin powder
  7. 1 tsp coriander powder
  8. 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
  9. 2 tsp chili powder
  10. 1/2 turmeric powder
  11. 1 tsp garam masala
  12. 1/2 cup fresh cream/ Half & Half
  13. 1/2 cup chopped coriander
  14. salt to taste
  15. 3 tbsp oil

For kofta

  1. 4-5 medium boiled potatoes
  2. 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  3. 1/2 cup paneer grated
  4. 1/2 cup finely cut carrot, beans and peas, boil and squeeze excess water
  5. 1 tbsp chopped cashew, almond and raisins
  6. 1 tsp cumin powder
  7. 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  8. 1/2 cup corn starch
  9. Oil for fry

Method:

  1. Peel the potatoes and mash them, add salt to taste.
  2. Add grated paneer, boiled veggie, chopped nuts, chilies, cumin powder and bread crumbs one by one.
  3. Mix that well and make a small balls out of that mix, roll or coat the balls in corn starch.
  4. Then deep fry in hot oil until slightly golden brown color and drain and keep aside.
  5. On the other side, heat pan and dry fry onion till the row smell disappear. Then make a onion paste in grinder.
  6. Now heat oil in a pan and then add the onion paste, garlic, ginger paste and fry for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Reduce the heat to medium, then add tomato puree mix it well and fry till oil separates from gravy.(it will take about 15-20 minutes)
  8. Then add all the spices cumin powder, coriander powder, chili powder, turmeric, masala and salt.fry for 2 minutes.
  9. Finally add half&half long with some water. Bring it to boil and stir occasionally.
  10. Garnish with finely chopped coriander and grated paneer.
  11. When you are serving, put the kofta in gravy and serve.

Malai Paneer


Malai Paneer

Preparation & Cooking: 35-45 mins
Serves persons: 4-5

Ingredients:

  1. 250 gms Cottage cheese/paneer
  2. 1 medium onion, chopped
  3. 4 medium tomatoes, chopped
  4. 1 tsp ginger paste
  5. 1 tsp garlic paste
  6. 2 green chilies
  7. 2 tbsp cashew nuts fine powder
  8. 1 tsp cumin powder
  9. 1 tsp coriander powder
  10. 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
  11. 2 tsp chili powder
  12. 1/2 turmeric powder
  13. 1 tsp garam masala
  14. 2 tsp kasuri menthi
  15. 1/2 cup fresh cream/ Half & Half
  16. 1/2 cup chopped coriander
  17. salt to taste
  18. 3 tbsp oil

Method:

  1. Cut paneer into small size cubes and shallow fry them. Keep it aside.
  2. Heat oil in pan and fry onion, garlic, ginger, chili till the row smell disappear.
  3. Then add the tomatoes and fry for 10 minutes till they are cooked.
  4. Then cool it and then grind to a puree.
  5. Then add all the spices cumin powder, coriander powder, chili powder, turmeric, masala, salt and cashew nut powder to puree.
  6. Cook it in low heat for 5 minutes. Now add half&half along with some water. Bring it to boil and stir occasionally.
  7. Then add crushed kasuri menthi and mix well.
  8. Garnish with finely chopped coriander and grated paneer.
  9. When you are serving, put the paneer in gravy and serve.

Palak Paneer


Palak Paneer

Preparation & Cooking: 35-40 mins
Serves: 4-5 persons

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup paneer
  2. 1 bunch Spinach
  3. 1 onion
  4. 1 inch ginger
  5. 2-3 green chilies.
  6. 10 Cashew nuts
  7. 1/2 sp mustard seeds
  8. 1/2 inch cinnamon
  9. 2 cloves
  10. 1 cardamom
  11. salt to taste
  12. 1 tsp sugar
  13. 1/2 cup Cream or 1 cup milk
  14. 2tbsp oil

Method:

  1. Cook the palak (spinach) for about 5 minutes in boiling water and grind it to fine paste after it is cold.
  2. Heat pan dry roast Cinnamon,clove and cardamom, and powdered this using a hand mortar and pestle in to a fine powder.
  3. Now shallow fry the onion in same pan. Grind onion, chilies, ginger and salt.keep that aside.
  4. Lightly saute paneer cubes in one teaspoon of oil till they are golden brown.
  5. Heat the oil in a pan and add the musterd seeds. Wait till it crackles and now add onion paste.Saute till the raw smell disappears.
  6. Add spinach paste, a pinch of masala powder and cashew nut powder. cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Now add milk/cream and mix well.cook for 10-15 minuts.
  8. Just before serving add paneer cubes.
  9. Serve hot with roti,paratha, pulka and naan.

Dum Aloo


Dum Aloo

Preparation & Cooking: 35-45 mins
Serves persons: 4-5

Ingredients:

  1. 10-12 boiled potatoes
  2. 2 big onion grated
  3. 4 tomatoes, chopped
  4. 1 inch ginger sliced
  5. 2 garlic cloves
  6. 2 green chilies
  7. 1 inch cinnamon
  8. 1 tsp cumin powder
  9. 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  10. 1 pods cardamom
  11. 4 cloves
  12. 2 tsp chili powder
  13. 1/2 turmeric powder
  14. 1 tsp garam masala
  15. 2 tbsp yogurt
  16. 1/2 cup chopped coriander
  17. salt to taste
  18. 1 tsp sugar
  19. 3 tbsp oil

Method:

  1. Peel the boiled potato, keep aside.
  2. Grind the cloves, cardamoms, cinnamon, chilies, ginger and garlic to a paste.
  3. Heat oil in pan and fry the grated onion and grind paste for few minutes.
  4. Add tomato and stir for 10-15 minutes till this masala leaves oil.
  5. Then add salt, turmeric powder, chili powder , garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, sugar. Continue to fry for 2-3 minutes.
    6. Then add 1 cup water and yogurt, mix it and cook it for 10 mins.
  6. Then add potato and mix well.
  7. Garnish with finely chopped coriander.

Chilly Paneer Gravy


Chilly Paneer Gravy

Preparation & Cooking: 35-40 mins
Serves: 4-5 persons

Ingredients:

  1. 250 gms paneer
  2. 3 tbsp corn flour
  3. 2 green capsicum (square cut)
  4. 1 onion (square cut)
  5. 1 onion chopped
  6. 2 tsp ginger and garlic paste
  7. 2 tomatoes chopped
  8. 3-4 green chilies.
  9. 2 tsp red chilies crushed
  10. 2 tbsp chili powder
  11. 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  12. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  13. 4 tbsp tomato sauce
  14. 1 tbsp soya sauce
  15. chopped coriander
  16. salt to taste
  17. 1tsp sugar
  18. 1/2 cup water
  19. 3tbsp oil

Method:

  1. Heat 2 tsp oil in pan and shallow fry paneer and keep aside.
  2. In same pan fry squared shaped capsicum and onion separately keep aside.
  3. In other pan heat oil, add cumin seeds and chopped onions.Fry 2mins.
  4. Add garlic, ginger paste saute till the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add chopped tomato, fry still they become paste.
  6. Now add all the spices like crushed chilly powder, chilly powder,turmeric powder and salt.
  7. Add soya sauce, tomato sauce and green chilies, Mix them well.
  8. Put some more water and cook for 4-5 mins till you get sauce consistency.
  9. Mix corn starch in 4tbsp water, pour into sauce ,stir it and cook 2 more min's.
  10. Add capsicum, onion and paneer, mix it well.
  11. Serve Chilly paneer with roti,naan and fried rice.

Chole


Panjabi chole

Preparation & Cooking: 35-40 mins
Serves: 4-5 persons

Ingredients:

  1. 11/2 cup chick peas (chole), soaked overnight
  2. 1 onion chopped
  3. 2 tomatoes chopped
  4. 2-3 green chilies.
  5. 3 tsp ginger and garlic paste
  6. 2 tsp chili powder
  7. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  8. 1 tsp cumin powder
  9. 1 tsp coriander powder
  10. 1/2tsp amchoor powder
  11. 1 tsp garam masala
  12. 2 tsp cumin seeds
  13. 4 tsp tomato sauce
  14. chopped coriander
  15. salt to taste
  16. 1 tea bag
  17. 1 tsp sugar(optional)
  18. 3tsp oil for cooking

Method:

  1. Cook the chick peas along with tea bag, turmeric and salt in pressure cooker.
  2. Heat oil in pan, Add cumin seeds and allow them to splutter.Add onion, ginger garlic paste and green chilies. Stir it well.
  3. Add chopped tomato fry for 5 minutes and then add all the dry spices fry for 2 minutes(chilly powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, amchur powder, garam masala)
  4. Add chick peas cooked water the gravy and Cook until it starts thickening.
  5. Add cooked chick peas cook well.
  6. Finally garnish with cilantro.

Rajma


Rajma

Preparation & Cooking: 35-40 mins
Serves: 4-5 persons

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups Kidney beans
  2. 2 finely chopped Onions
  3. 4-5 finely chopped Tomato
  4. 2 tsp Ginger paste
  5. 2-3 green chilies thinly sliced
  6. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  7. 1 tsp cumin powder
  8. 1 tsp coriander powder
  9. salt to taste
  10. 3tbsp oil
  11. finely chopped coriander

    12. For masala

  12. 1 inch cinnamon stick
  13. 2-3 cloves
  14. 1 whole green cardamoms
  15. 3-4 black pepper

Method:

  1. Soak the kidney beans overnight. Boil rajma in pressure cooker till they become soft.
  2. Grind all masala ingredient.
  3. Heat oil in pan and add onion, chili and ginger, fry for 5mins.
  4. Now add cumin-coriander powder.Add chopped tomato fry till it paste.
  5. Add salt, turmeric, red chilly powder and grind masala.
  6. Finally add rajma, cook for 10mins.
  7. Garnish with chopped coriander.
  8. Rajma goes well with pulca and rice.

Okra Masala


Okra Masala

Preparation & Cooking: 20-25 mins
Serves: 4-5 persons

Ingredients:

  1. 250 gms Okra or bhindi sliced
  2. 1 medium onion
  3. 1 small tomato
  4. 2 cloves garlic
  5. 1 tsp cumin seeds
  6. 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  7. 2 tsp chili powder
  8. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  9. 1/2 tsp garam masala
  10. 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
  11. 1 tsp lemon juice
  12. finely Chopped coriander
  13. salt to taste
  14. 2 tbsp oil

Method:

  1. Heat the pan and dry fry the bhindi in medium flame for 5 mins.
  2. Then put 1 tsp of oil, fry for 5 more mins and keep aside.
  3. Now in the same pan dry fry onion, garlic, cumin seeds and then add all ingredient except oil, mustard seed in grinder, make smooth paste.
  4. Heat oil in pan, add masala paste fry till leave the oil.
  5. Then add fried bhindi to it, mix it well.
  6. Finally garnish with chopped coriander.

Masala Egg


Masala Egg

Preparation & Cooking: 15-20 mins
Serves 3-4 persons

Ingredients:

  1. 4 eggs
  2. 1 big onion chopped
  3. 1 medium tomato chopped
  4. 1 small capsicum sliced
  5. 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  6. 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  7. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  8. 1 tsp red chili powder
  9. 1/2 tsp garam masala
  10. Pinch of pepper powder
  11. Finely chopped coriander
  12. Salt to taste
  13. 2 tbsp oil

Method:

  1. Heat oil in pan, Add cumin seeds and allow them to splutter.
  2. Add onion fry till it become soft. Then add tomato, capsicum fry for 5 mins.
  3. Then add salt, turmeric powder, chili powder, garam masala and mix well.
  4. Now crack eggs,distribute evenly over curry mixture. Reduce the heat to low, cover the lid and cook till egg is cooked.
  5. Garnish with chopped coriander and pepper powder.

Bengan Curry


Bengan Curry

Preparation & Cooking: 35-40 mins
Serves 3-4 persons

Ingredients:

  1. 5 medium brinjals
  2. 1 small onion chopped
  3. 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  4. 3 tsp oil
  5. 2-3 curry leaves

For filling

  1. 1 medium onion chopped
  2. 3 cloves garlic
  3. 2 tbsp shredded coconut
  4. 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  5. 3 tsp chili powder
  6. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  7. 1/2 cumin seeds
  8. 1 tsp garam masala
  9. 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  10. 1 tsp grated jaggery
  11. 1 small ball sizes tamarind
  12. Chopped coriander
  13. salt to taste

Method:

  1. Fry sesame seeds, shredded coconuts separately till they turn slight brown in color.
  2. In the same pan fry onion, garlic till the row smell disappear.
  3. Now add coconut, sesame seeds, onion, garlic and other all ingredients in filling, grind and make a smooth paste.
  4. Wash the brinjals and cut off the stems. Slice them and make a four cuts horizontally and vertically.
  5. Stuff the paste into brinjal and keep aside.
  6. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, let them splutter.
  7. Add chopped onion saute till translucent.
  8. Then add stuffed brinjal and fry for 2 minutes.
  9. Add 1/2 cup water, mix it and cover the lid. cook for 8-10 minutes till brinjals are cooked.
  10. Garnish with chopped coriander.

Mutter paneer


Mutter Paneer

Preparation & Cooking: 35-45 mins
Serves persons: 4-5

Ingredients:

  1. 250 gms Cottage cheese/paneer
  2. 1 cup boiled peas/mutter
  3. 1 big onion chopped
  4. 2 tomatoes, chopped
  5. 1 inch ginger sliced
  6. 2 tsp garlic cloves sliced
  7. 1 green chilies
  8. 1 inch cinnamon
  9. 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  10. 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  11. 1 pods cardamom
  12. 2 cloves
  13. 2 black pepper corns
  14. 2 tsp chili powder
  15. 1/2 turmeric powder
  16. 1 tsp garam masala
  17. 1 tsp kasuri menthi
  18. 2 tbsp yogurt
  19. 1/4 cup fresh cream/ Half & Half/milk
  20. 1/2 cup chopped coriander
  21. salt to taste
  22. 1 tsp sugar
  23. 3 tbsp oil

Method:

  1. Cut paneer into small size cubes and shallow fry them. Keep it aside.
  2. Dry roast cinnamon, black pepper corn, cloves, cardamom, cumin seeds, coriander seeds for 2 mins and make smooth powder in a grinder.
  3. Heat 1 tbsp oil in pan and fry onion, garlic, ginger, chili till the row smell disappear.
  4. Then add the tomatoes and fry for 5 minutes till they are cooked.
  5. Then cool it and then add salt, turmeric powder, chili powder , sugar and grind to make smooth puree.
  6. Now again heat oil in pan and then add puree, fry it until oil separates from gravy. when it become thick paste, add dry roasted powder,garam masala, peas and mix it well.
  7. Then add 1 cup water and yogurt, mix it and cook it for 2 mins.
  8. Now add half&half bring it to boil and stir occasionally.
  9. Then add crushed kasuri menthi and mix well.
  10. Garnish with finely chopped coriander and grated paneer.
  11. When you are serving, put the paneer in gravy and serve.

Pitla Recipe


Pitla

Preparation & Cooking: 15-20 mins
Serves 4-5 persons

Ingredients:

  1. 3 tbsp besan
  2. 1 medium finely Chopped onion
  3. 1 tsp green chilly paste
  4. 1 tsp garlic paste
  5. 1 tsp lemon juice
  6. 3-4 curry leaves
  7. 2 tsp finely chopped coriander leaves
  8. 1/2 tsp mustard seed
  9. 1 tp cumin seeds
  10. 1/2 tsp turmeric
  11. salt to taste
  12. 1/2 tsp sugar(optional)
  13. 2tbsp tsp oil
  14. 5 cup water

Method:

  1. Heat the oil and add mustard seed, cumin seed and curry leaves.
  2. When they start cracking add garlic paste and chopped onion and fry till they become tender.
  3. Add green chilly paste, salt, sugar and turmeric and fry for a minute.
  4. Now add water, when water start boiling add besan, stir it quickly till it settle.
  5. Finally add coriander leaves and lemon juice and lower the heat keep it for 5 minute.

  6. Pitla goes well with both Chapati and Pain Rice.
